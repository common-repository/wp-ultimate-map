Place a map on your wordpress website with custom markers , infowindows and Routes.

== Description ==

WP Ultimate Map is wordress plugin which allows to group places with unique icons. You can group routes as well,
You can place multiple places on map , you can draw multiple routes on map , You can draw a route and attach the places to that perticular route ,
It is very eye catching display of places and routes on the map.
Filter the frontend view of the map according to custom taxonomies.
Seo friendly places creation , search engines can easily crawl information about places.
Placing a map on your website is very easy you can simply use a shortcode [umap]. 
All this functions helps you to attach your visitors Geographically with your website.

A few notes about the sections above:

*   Its very easy to place map on your website.
*   Its very easy to place markers and add places to that marker.
*   Drawing a routes by places.
*   manually drawing a routes on map.
*   Displaying a information about the place in infowindow.
*   Filtering the places by the markers.
*   Filtering the routes by the color.
*   Seo friendly

1. Download the plugins
2. upload and Extract files to /wp-content/plugins/ directory
3. Go to backend and activate plugin and you are ready to go.
